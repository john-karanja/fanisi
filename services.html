Our Services

Hr Consulting

Fanisi HR focuses on HR Administration delivery areas like developing employee contracts, personnel files, staff correspondence letters, documentation, statutory regulations and database management, to evaluate an organization's operational policies on human resource. We take a step further and conduct HR audits, engage in document development, streamline record keeping including personnel information, leave schedules, job descriptions for all positions and organizational charts. We make sure that documentation is an integral part of this process, enabling companies to keep records that can be traced back. Fanisi is assisting organizations to set up, develop and improve their HR administration.

Performance Management

We work with organisations to implement Performance Management Systems. With Performance Management, managers are able to develop and communicate clear job outcomes to the organisation's employees. These outcomes or job expectations are cascaded down from the organisation's strategic goals and plans. Managers ensure that the employees know how all their jobs are designed to meet common organisational goals. Performance Management is useful in today's work environment which is knowledge or information based. This is an environment where employees understand and own their contribution, own the process and remain motivated to perform at their best. Performance Management has helped managers working with a generation that is now joining the job market, which values flexibility, want to feel valued and dislikes heavy handed micromanaging.

We work with organisations in this process to:

  • Clarify Job expectations
  • Agree on meaningful performance measures
  • Induction and training, coaching and mentoring
  • Collecting realistic data and analysing the same
  • Interpret this data for decision making
