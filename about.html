About
Fanisi

Fanisi is a HR consulting and management company that guides enterprises in setting up management and development of their workforce. We support Small and Medium Enterprises to excel in improving the people component of their businesses and organizations by building human capital through developing employee related policies and practices, staff training and development, incentive programs, talent acquisition and retention.

We are at hand to ensure that organizations recruit the right personnel for specified positions. Further, we empower organizations to conduct induction by outlining the guiding principles and professional ethics expected at the work place, define job roles and communicate the terms and conditions of the engagement to enable personnel fit into the organizational culture seamlessly. Where necessary, Fanisi identifies gaps in skills and develops tailored training to bridge these gaps.

Our scope also encompasses delivering soft skills for handling relations between management and employees, by improving communication in order to link the various departments and outlining the different roles they play towards the common good. We are however, instrumental in breaking down the individual roles and showing how they work towards the realization of the greater goal.

Management & Staff

Kevinah Nyambura

Fanisi is headed by Kevina Nyambura; a graduate in education from Kenyatta University and she also holds a Masters in Marketing and HR from Middlesex University, London. Her focus is on Small and Medium Enterprises spanning across different sectors. Further, she undertakes strategy, development and implementation, with the overall objective of establishing a high performance and reliable workforce for SMEs.

With over ten years work experience, Kevina has a diverse human resource background forged on solid foundations of experience and numerous trainings that have placed her as the brains behind Fanisi HR solutions. She is a member of the Institute of Human Resource Management (IHRM) and is well versed on industry fundamentals and best practices.
